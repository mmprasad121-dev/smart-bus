About the System

Government bus passengers often face safety challenges, including delayed emergency response and lack of real-time location tracking. This intelligent system ensures that emergency alerts are instantly sent while sharing live bus location, enabling transport authorities to act faster and improve passenger trust.

How It Works

Our system integrates emergency buttons, GPS tracking, and a central control system. Here's the step-by-step process:

  1. A passenger presses the emergency button installed inside the bus.
  2. The system immediately triggers a buzzer and visual alerts within the bus.
  3. Simultaneously, the GPS module records the live location of the bus.
  4. The control room and transport authorities receive real-time notifications with exact location details.
  5. Authorities can respond quickly, ensuring passenger safety and timely assistance.

Prototype Overview
